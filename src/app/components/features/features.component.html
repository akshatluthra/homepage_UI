How Digivante usability testing works

Hyper-targeted

We work with you to identify your target demographic, then we identify people within the Digivante community that match those requirements.

Prioritisation

We categorise issues by severity, guided by Nielsen’s industry standard principles.

Real reactions

We capture pre- and post- experience descriptions of user reactions and feelings.

Demographic data

We create detailed usability reports including the number of users and their demographics.

Screen recording

We provide video evidence to support the usability reports.

Positive experience

Our testers don’t just capture negative issues: they also identify what’s effective and appealing in your site or app.

Quality assured

You receive moderated, prioritised
recommendations for change.

Detailed reports

Your report includes trust rating, net promoter
score and web usability score.
